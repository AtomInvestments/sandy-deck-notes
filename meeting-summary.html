Roundtable feedback.

Key Themes from the Roundtable.

The overall sentiment from the room was positive. The consensus was that Sandy's core story is compelling, the IP is real, and the case study demonstrates meaningful value. The feedback was not centered around the viability of the company, but rather around how the value proposition is packaged and communicated to prospective buyers.

Roundtable Conclusion

The room was largely aligned that Sandy has the story, the technology, and the proof points. The primary opportunity is refining how those strengths are communicated. Tightening the financial narrative, leading through the right organizational entry point, and making the dashboard a tangible part of the product story were the most commonly cited recommendations.
