i. The read

My take.

A few honest lines

Sandy's pitch is in the right shape. The story tracks, the IP is real, and the case study sells itself. The packaging is two notches off — and all three problems are fixable by Monday.

One — the CFO vocabulary isn't there. DSO, net collection rate, gross collection rate, reimbursement timeframe. Until those four words sit on every slide, the room hears a pitch — not a P&L delta.

Two — the entry door is wrong. David said it three different ways: don't walk into the C-suite at a large Epic hospital cold. Walk into the physicians' org first and let it pull you upstream.

Three — the analytics dashboard that anchors the whole pitch hasn't shipped. Until it does, every "we can forecast revenue" line is a promise instead of a proof slide. Ship it next week and the deck flips.

Fix all three and the same room votes yes.
